From Profound Loss to Systematic Hope

This is the story of how one parent's devastating loss became a framework for preventing future tragedies through evidence-based cooperation and abundance-based governance.

The Loss

"I will not die without changing the things I think had even a fraction of causality in the loss my baby had."

A parent's promise made in the deepest grief becomes the foundation for systematic change.

The Analysis

"People are good. Systems are broken. We can fix the systems."

Systematic analysis reveals that most tragedies result from systemic failures, not individual failings.

The Discovery

Mathematical proof that abundance already exists; only political will is needed.

The Eternal Promise

This work extends beyond any individual life or organization. It is a promise made to the children we've lost, the children still living, the children yet to be born, and all who suffer from preventable causes.

"The only way to honor those we've lost is to build a world where fewer people have to experience such loss. Every day we don't do this work, more people join the ranks of the grieving. Every day we do this work, fewer people will have to."

In memory of those who should still be here. In service of those who still can be saved.
